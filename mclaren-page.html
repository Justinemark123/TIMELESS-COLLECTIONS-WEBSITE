Engineered for absolute superiority and driven by the legendary rivalry of Ayrton Senna and Alain Prost, the MP4/4 is the most successful Formula 1 car of all time. In the 1988 season, it achieved the impossible, winning 15 out of 16 races and capturing every pole position but one. Its low-slung, carbon-fiber chassis paired with a lethal Honda V6 turbo engine created a machine so balanced and efficient that it led 97% of all laps that year. More than just a winner, the MP4/4 redefined the limits of motorsport engineering, cementing McLaren’s status as a racing titan and providing the ultimate canvas for the greatest drivers in history to achieve immortality.
