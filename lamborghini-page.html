A masterclass in raw aggression and aerospace-inspired design, the Aventador is the ultimate personification of the Lamborghini DNA. Named after a legendary bull of the Spanish Corrida, it was engineered to be a fighter, featuring a revolutionary carbon-fiber monocoque and a screaming, naturally aspirated 6.5-liter V12 engine. Its sharp, geometric lines and iconic scissor doors command attention, while its lightning-fast ISR transmission and visceral exhaust note provide an experience that is as brutal as it is beautiful.
